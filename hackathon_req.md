Digital Frontier

Design a digital solution that protects Tunisians from real cyber threats.

OpBrief: Your challenge is to design and build a digital solution that increases cyber awareness and helps reduce vulnerability to online threats among Tunisians.
The Situation

Cyber threats are increasing in Tunisia. From phishing messages and fake online shops to social engineering and identity theft, many citizens are exposed to digital risks without fully understanding how to protect themselves.

EXAMPLES OF THREAT VECTORS DETECTED IN THE FIELD:
Phishing & SMS Scams

Deceptive communications mimicking trusted entities (Poste, Banks, Gov).
Social Media Fraud

Marketplace scams, hijacked accounts, and fake seller profiles.
WhatsApp & Mobile

Verification code theft and impersonation attacks targeting contacts.
Identity Theft

Unauthorized use of personal data to commit financial fraud.
Privacy Breaches

Unknowingly sharing sensitive data via insecure platforms.
Weak Credentials

Password reuse and lack of MFA adoption across critical services.
Fake websites

Deceptive websites designed to steal personal or financial information.
Malicious Softwares

Software designed to harm or exploit devices and networks.
Mission Directives

Create a solution that educates, empowers, or protects individuals and/or small businesses in Tunisia from real cyber threats.

    Raise awareness about common cyber risks.
    Help users recognize suspicious behavior or content.
    Provide clear guidance on prevention and safe practices.
    Encourage safer digital habits
